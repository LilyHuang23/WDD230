Room Choice

Standard Room

It is likely the same as a queen or a single room, great for a solo traveler or a couple.

Deluxe Room

These rooms are typically equipped for groups who need more space with slightly upgraded amenities or a nicer view., like a couple or small family.

Joint Room

Two rooms that share a common wall but no connecting door.

Joint rooms are meant for families with younger children who may be old enough to stay in their own space, but not too far from their parents.
