Target Audience

Personas

(1) Business people wants to publicize their business will come to this site.

(2) Local people or tourism want to know where have activity in Idaho.

Scenarios

(1) Madison county is going to have a county fair on 8/24/2022, Idaho Travel site will be the best website for the county to promote.

(2) Local students are holding an art show that wants to show their artwork, they can post this information on the Idaho Travel site.

(3) Kelin starts her small business but doesn't know where to boost her business, she can make a commercial post and link to her website.

(4) Yellowstone is a beautiful place to visit from April to October. Airb&b owner wants to provide their rental information, they can share their rental info on the website.

(5) Andy is planning to come to Idaho next week, he hopes he can visit a website that includes recommendations of where to visit and what activity is currently hosting.
